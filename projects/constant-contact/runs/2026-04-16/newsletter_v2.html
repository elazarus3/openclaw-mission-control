Quick Q&A

"Can I drink alcohol while on Zepbound or Wegovy?"

Short answer: Yes — but be thoughtful about it. GLP-1s slow gastric emptying, which means alcohol absorbs more slowly but its effects can feel less predictable. Some patients find they're more sensitive to drinks they used to handle fine. High-sugar mixed drinks can also counteract some of the metabolic benefits. If you do drink, go slow and stay hydrated. And if you're on diabetes-specific formulations, talk to us before combining with alcohol.
