Quick Q&A

"Can I drink alcohol while on Zepbound or Wegovy?"

Short answer: Yes — but be thoughtful about it. GLP-1s slow gastric emptying, meaning alcohol absorbs more slowly and its effects can feel less predictable. High-sugar mixed drinks can counteract some of the metabolic benefits. Go slow, stay hydrated, and talk to us before combining with alcohol if you're on a diabetes-specific formulation.
